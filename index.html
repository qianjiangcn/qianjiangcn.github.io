About

I am a third-year Ph.D. Candidate in the department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). I obtained my B.S. degree in Electronic Engineering in 2019 from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China (UESTC).

I am interested in research relating to machine learning and computer vision. My current research involves transfer learning, multi-task learning, AutoML, multimodal learning, large-scale vision and language models.

Research Interests

Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing, Multimodal Learning.
